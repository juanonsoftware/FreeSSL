Welcome to FreeSSL support site

This site helps you to validate your FREE SSL certificate quickly. Below are steps or you can watch a video here

  1. Create your own domain name. If you do not have one, try a FREE subdomain like this freessl.mooo.com with freedns.afraid.org.
  2. Put A address to same IP with freessl.abizvn.com (do ping freessl.abizvn.com then you will see it). Now you see the maintenance page when access to your domain.
  3. Use a client to generate your free SSL certificate, such as Crypt::Le
  4. At verification step, upload the validation file to our ftp server with info here
  5. Once it's completed, you can continue to complete your own SSL.
  6. Congrats! It's all done, you now have a valid SSL cert of your domain.
  7. You can configure your domain with the certificate, your side is ready.
